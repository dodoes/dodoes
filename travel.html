Travel Blog

2016

Iceland

Wonderful nature and stunning views that took my breath away. Iceland is a country of mountains, geysers, and small horses. Definitely, must place to visit. Yet, it is quite expensive. Plus, all the beautiful and famous places are located pretty far away from each other. Therefore you will probably need a car.

Alaska

Glorious nature and stunning views that took my breath away. Alaska is a country of high mountains, big bears, and cold glaciers. Definitely, must place to visit. Yet, it is quite expensive. Must be remembered that all the beautiful places are located pretty far away from each other. Therefore you will probably need a car.

Israel

Marvelous nature and stunning views that took my breath away. Israel is a country of friendly people, long history, and delicious food. Definitely, must place to visit. Yet, it is quite expensive. All the famous places are located pretty close to each other. Therefore, it easy to see the whole Israel in several days.

Poland

Amazing nature and stunning architecture that took my breath away. Poland is a country of mountains, old and modern buildings and a lot of food. Definitely, must place to visit. It is not expensive to go there. In Poland, you will manage to see everything you want. The country combines sea and mountains, western and eastern cultures, honor of history and commitment to the future.
